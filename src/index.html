About Me

I'm Giulia Crivello a young holistic therapist.

Since I was kid I've always found fascinating the alternative healing techniques and disciplines. Driven by this passion I have taken the path of studies in this field and successfully graduated at Turin «Due Cieli» Popular University.

My primary goal is to help people regain their physical and emotinal balance.
In order to get the best results for my clients, I stick to a strict work ethic.

My working philosophy is based on the knowledge that every person is unique, as well as their hopes, dreams, fears and difficulties.
Because of this I aim to create treatments that adapt perfectly to the personality and needs of the customer.

Experience

My career started when I still had to finish my studies, over five years ago.

My first area of interest has been the holistic accompaniment to pregnancy : I've hold prenatal classes and helped new mothers to live their pregnancy in a serene and natural way.

In 2012 I've co-founded the holistic centre «Light Blossoms» based in Turin, where I held meditation classes and one-on-one lessons, practiced massage and Bach flowers therapies. In the same year I've started collaborating with various holistic associations both as teacher and therapist.

As a freelance I treated the elderly, children with relationship issues and adults in search of equilibrium.
